Jungle is Massive


This is a simple text based game where you venture through the jungle to unravel its mysteries .


Created as a simple test of basic abilities when re-introducing myself to programming , a place to start and get myself comfortable before going on to learn new things .


The story ventures into two different sides of the jungle , where you encounter different types of foes on either side . You can find items on different paths that can assist you , and they will be vital to reaching the end .


Don 't be afraid to go backwards every once in a while , it could surprise you .
